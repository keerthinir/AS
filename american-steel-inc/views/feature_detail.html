Standard Roof Style

The classic standard style roof is our most economical option. This standard style gives your garage the rounded, curved look, and comes in a variety of sizes. The carport comes with a standard horizontal roof where the roof panels run parallel to the side of the carport. Like all our products, they are reinforced with center and corner braces for stability and strength.

Choose from a regular carport, partial enclosed, to a triple wide garage. Our roll up and overhead doors come in a variety of sizes ranging from a single door to a double door. We also have walk in doors and windows available. Visit our accessories page for more information. Mix and match the roof, sides, ends, and trim colors at no additional charge choosing from our standard 12 color choices. We can customize a standard style garage to fit your everyday storage needs.
